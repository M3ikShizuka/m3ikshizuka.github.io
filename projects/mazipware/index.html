MAZIPWARE

Brief description of the developed system

MAZIPWARE is a company specializing in research, development and modification of software. This company develops and sells its own digital products using the SaaS model. Each customer has their own account through which they can access all the products they purchase. Distribution of products is done through the client application. The client application allows customers to install, update, delete a digital product, check the integrity of the installed product and receive information about the current status of the product.
